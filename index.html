Investor FAQ

Q. Why is the 18-month cycle important?

Capital velocity is our primary risk mitigation tool. While traditional funds lock capital for 5-7 years, our 18-month "Short-Duration" strategy allows investors to realize an ~1.8x Equity Multiple and recycle capital quickly, minimizing exposure to long-term market cycles.

Q. How do you justify the aggressive exit pricing?

Exit pricing is supported by recent comparable sales on E Joshua Tree Lane ($17.0M) and E Meadowlark Lane ($14.27M). Our 9,000+ SF designs with modern-industrial architecture are positioned competitively within demonstrated market parameters.

Q. What is the proposed deal structure?

We propose a 7% Preferred Return to investors, followed by an 80/20 split until a 20% IRR hurdle is met. Above that hurdle, profits are split 50/50, aligning incentives to maximize the total portfolio value.

Q. Why Hickok Homes for a Paradise Valley project?

With 120+ years of development experience and over 7,000 living units built, Hickok Homes brings unmatched expertise in luxury residential construction. James Hickok's commitment to bespoke design and the family's reputation for quality craftsmanship ensures each project exceeds expectations.
